Algorithms for Partially Observable Stochastic Games

Introduction

Solving partially observable stochastic games (POSG) involve decision making among multiple players where only only imperfect information is available, making them particularly challenging and relevant for complex real-world scenarios. I will be testing and experimenting with algorithms for solving such games, especially those with continuous spaces that likely makes classic tree search methods ineffective.

I started working on this project recently and I'm still in the exploration stage. Super excited to work with VAMPIR Lab and the brilliant people here!
